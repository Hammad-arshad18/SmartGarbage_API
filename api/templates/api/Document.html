Project Objectives

The primary objective is to resolve the issue of improper waste management in Pakistan. For this purpose, the proposed solution will bring all the stakeholders on one platform where they will work together for achieving the mentioned goal. The deep-learning-based intelligent algorithm will help in the identification of heaps of garbage in the city. Moreover, it will also improve the performance of janitorial staff and the concerned authorities by evaluating their performance. In addition to this, based on the system-generated reports, the government officials can perceive the future needs and consequently, plan for waste management in the future. Resultantly, creating an environment and climate-friendly happy lifestyle for the citizens of Pakistan.
